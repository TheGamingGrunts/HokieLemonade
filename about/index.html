Our Origins

More About Hokie Lemonade

Hokie Lemonade was devised in a classroom as part of a business assignment. The objective of the assignment was to prepare the class to enter the business world and what better was of doing that then by making us create and operate our own small business.

Our project finally kicked off on September 1, 2014 when the first lines of code for the website were written and our business plan began to take shape. Over the course of the coming days, we began to finalize our plan and gather the necessary resources in order to operate the lemonade stand

About

Hokie Lemonade is a project run and maintained by Virginia Tech students. The goal of this project is to raise as much money as possible to privide a better lifestyle to kids living in Nepal by selling lemonade and other food products.
